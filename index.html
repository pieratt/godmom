12/23 — First post

For 20 years I've been finding patterns in founders' visions and translating them into designs. For 10 years I've been finding patterns in spiritual visions and translating them into designs.

In the 7th year, I found a pattern of patterns. It is both backwards compatible and new, it speaks the same language as the internet and the I Ching. To understand its rules is to have the sensation of sharing a game with our ancestors, a binary analog to the perennial philosophy — I believe it can offer ancient inner truths a bridge to our accelerating landscape.

This is the work of a lifetime and none of it is complete. Thank you for your patience with me as I try to share with you the most beautiful pattern I've ever seen, what I believe to be a display of elegance 5,000 years in the making.
